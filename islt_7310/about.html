My story

My name is Haleigh Lewis. I am currently a sophomore at Mizzou studying psychology and will graduate in the spring of 2024. Although my major is psychology, fashion has always been a huge passion of mine. I have worked in retail since I turned 16 and am currently a part-time manager at Express, working as a stylist and as an associate at PacSun.

These two businesses sell very different styles and have the ability to help customers with both day to day dressing as well as business dressing. I mainly specialize in street wear however I can appreciate and help with fancier outfits. However, the most common problem I am asked to help with at either company is, what matches what, whether that be a suit and a tie or a pair of jeans and a top. With all of this experience, I have become quite skilled with the art of fashion. With fashion, there is a quote that I live by from Miuccia Prada:

"What you wear is how you present yourself to the world, especially today, when human contacts are so quick. Fashion is instant language."
